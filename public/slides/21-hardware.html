NVIDIA, AMD, Apple, Broadcom queueing for the same N3/N2 wafers. Consumer GPUs, game consoles, phones all wait in line.

What this looks like for normal people

  • RAM upgrades on PCs: DDR5 retail kits roughly doubled YoY through late 2025.
  • Consumer GPUs (NVIDIA RTX 50-series, AMD 9000) — limited supply at launch (Jan 2025), MSRP rarely available.
  • SSD prices rising after hitting multi-year lows in 2023, reversing a long deflationary trend.

Why

The fabs that make memory for your laptop are the same fabs making HBM stacks for H100/B200 GPUs. Hyperscalers will pay whatever it takes. Consumers won't. So the consumer market gets what's left.
