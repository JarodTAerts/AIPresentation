what to take away

If you remember five things

01
The math is simple, the scale is wild.
Matrix multiplication supplies the scale; attention supplies context. Training adjusts billions of weights through next-token prediction and post-training.
02
The model is one box.
Tools, retrieval, safety filters, agent loops — that's where the practical magic lives.
03
Code went first because it has a grader.
Benchmarks moved quickly, but real productivity depends on task, user, codebase, tools, and evaluation.
04
AI makes building the exact tool you need far easier.
Describe it in plain language and almost anyone can spin up software shaped to their needs — so one person does more, and fewer are needed per task.
05
The bill is real.
Energy, concentrated capital, labor transitions, copyright, security, and accountability. Useful skepticism is a feature.
