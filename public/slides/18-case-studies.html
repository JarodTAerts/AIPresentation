Klarna

AI customer-service agent doing the work of ~700 full-time agents. Resolution times dropped from 11 min → under 2. Headcount fell from ~7,400 → 2,831 (2022 → end-2025, −62%).

Klarna press release (Feb 2024); Klarna 20-F (FY2025, filed Feb 2026).

Amazon (AWS)

Andy Jassy memo, June 2025 (per CNBC / Reuters): "We will need fewer people doing some of the jobs that are being done today." Layoffs across corporate roles followed.

Amazon internal memo, June 17 2025 (as reported).

Duolingo

"AI-first" reorg, April 2025. Contractors doing translatable work cut. Internal teams restructured around AI tooling.

Luis von Ahn all-hands email, April 2025.

Big Law / Big 4

Several firms publicly reducing first-year associate / staff-consultant intake — Allen & Overy, Goldman Sachs, KPMG cited AI explicitly.

FT, Reuters, Bloomberg 2024–2025 coverage.

The honest caveat. Klarna's CEO publicly acknowledged (May 2025) the AI-only push hit quality limits — though headcount kept falling through 2025. IBM's Krishna said ~200 HR jobs were already replaced by AI by early 2024, with plans to pause hiring on up to 7,800 back-office roles over five years — while overall headcount grew. Net pattern: fewer entry-level seats, more senior leverage.
