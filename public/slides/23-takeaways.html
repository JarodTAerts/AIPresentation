what to take away

If you remember five things

01
The math is simple, the scale is wild.
Modern AI = stacks of matrix multiplications. Trillions of numbers, trillions of training tokens.
02
The model is one box.
Tools, retrieval, safety filters, agent loops — that's where the practical magic lives.
03
Code went first because it has a grader.
Other knowledge work is now following — slowly at the senior end, fast at the junior end.
04
MCP + Skills = how chatbots become "your company's AI."
Worth understanding even if you'll never build one.
05
The bill is real.
Energy, hardware, wealth concentration, copyright, the entry-level career ladder. Useful skepticism is a feature.
