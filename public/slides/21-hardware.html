What this looks like for normal people

  • RAM upgrades on PCs: ~2× more expensive than a year ago.
  • Consumer GPUs (NVIDIA RTX 50-series, AMD 9000) — limited supply, MSRP rarely available.
  • SSDs are creeping back up after a decade of falling prices.

Why

The fabs that make memory for your laptop are the same fabs making HBM stacks for H100/B200 GPUs. Hyperscalers will pay whatever it takes. Consumers won't. So the consumer market gets what's left.
