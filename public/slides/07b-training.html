So how do billions of weights get learned?

Nobody types them in. Training repeatedly predicts the next token, measures the error, and uses that signal to adjust the model.

Step 01 · Gather
Build a training mixture
Combine and filter public, licensed, human-created, and synthetic data. Llama 3 disclosed about 15 trillion tokens — subword chunks, not words.
gather · clean · chunk into tokens
Step 02 · Predict
Predict what comes next
Give the model all earlier tokens and ask for the next one. It cannot look ahead.
"The cat sat on the" → predict " mat"
just: guess the next word
Step 03 · Adjust
Nudge the weights
Backpropagation assigns credit for the error; an optimizer nudges many weights toward a less-wrong prediction. Repeat across enormous batches.
measure the error · nudge the weights
Step 04 · Refine
Turn it into an assistant
Demonstrations teach instruction-following. Preference data, model feedback, and verifiable rewards shape helpfulness, safety, and reasoning.
coach it to be helpful & safe
⚡ Scale
Published frontier runs use tens of thousands of accelerators for weeks to months. Exact cost and data mixtures are usually undisclosed. Once training ends, deployed weights are normally frozen during a conversation.

Sources: Meta, The Llama 3 Herd of Models (2024) · OpenAI InstructGPT (2022) · Meta Llama 4 announcement (2025). Tokens ≠ words; pretraining ≠ post-training.
