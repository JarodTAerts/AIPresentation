One plausible path: cheaper software makes more tasks automatable.

Where software already coordinates work:

  • Banks — account systems, risk models, compliance, and human oversight.
  • Insurance — actuarial models, documents, and claims workflows.
  • Airlines, logistics, retail — pricing, routing, and inventory.
  • Professional and public services — document-heavy legal, accounting, HR, health, education, and government workflows.
1
