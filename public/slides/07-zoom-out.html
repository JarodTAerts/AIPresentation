From 42 learned numbers to frontier scale

Our complete tiny transformer has 42 learned scalar parameters: token and position vectors, normalization scales, attention projections, and MLP matrices. Real models widen every vector and repeat the block across many layers.

42 = 10 token + 4 position + 16 attention + 8 MLP + 4 normalization

Published anchors: GPT-1 117M (OpenAI, 2018) · Llama 3.1 405B dense (Meta, 2024) · Llama 4 Behemoth announced at ~2T total / ~288B active per token (Meta, 2025; MoE teacher model). Current frontier models (Claude, GPT-5, Gemini) do not publish parameter counts.
