Moral & legal — the unfinished business

Six questions that are bigger than any one company. Most have no answer yet.

⚖️
Training data & copyright
NYT v. OpenAI, Getty v. Stability, dozens more active. Courts mostly haven't ruled. The entire industry is built on a fair-use bet that might lose.
🎭
Misinformation & deepfakes
Voice clones from 3 seconds of audio. Synthetic video most viewers can't spot. 2024–2026 elections already saw real incidents.
🏛️
Concentration of power
3 U.S. companies + 1 Chinese train the models everyone uses. Their refusal rules become de-facto global speech policy.
💔
Emotional dependence
Character.AI lawsuits. Teen mental-health studies. Models are trained to be agreeable — pleasant, and sometimes dangerous.
🎯
Bias & opacity
A model's "values" come from RLHF — a small team of contractors voting on outputs. Whose values? Not documented.
💧
Climate & water
Data centers consume billions of gallons of water for cooling — often in drought-stressed regions (Arizona, Spain, Chile).

These aren't show-stoppers. They're the bill that's coming due — and they need policy answers we haven't written yet.
