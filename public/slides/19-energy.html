Energy — every query costs power.

Move the slider. A single query is small. A billion queries a day adds up.

~0.3 Wh per typical ChatGPT-class query (Epoch AI 2025 revision — 10× lower than the older 3 Wh estimate; long-context or reasoning tasks can exceed 5–40 Wh). U.S. home avg ≈ 30 kWh/day (EIA). Grid intensity ≈ 0.35 kg CO₂/kWh (EPA eGRID 2023).

Why it matters for utility bills: in PJM (Mid-Atlantic, ~67M residents), the 2025/26 capacity auction cleared 9× higher than the prior year — already adding ~$25–30/month to residential bills. ERCOT (Texas) has absorbed similar data-center load growth without a comparable price spike due to its different market design. Several states now let hyperscalers directly contract with nuclear & gas plants — at prices the public market can't match. Sources: PJM 2025/26 Base Residual Auction; SemiAnalysis "AI data center power bills"; EIA AEO 2026.
