Estimate: ~0.3 Wh for a typical GPT-4o-class query (Epoch AI, Feb 2025); heavy long-context/reasoning workloads can be much higher. Household and grid comparisons use U.S. averages.

Data centers affect bills through the grid, not through one prompt. In PJM, capacity prices jumped about 9× as data-center demand rose alongside plant retirements and transmission limits. Residential effects vary by utility — generally a few dollars per month, not a universal $25–30. Sources: official PJM 2025/26 Base Residual Auction report; EIA; utility/regulator estimates.
