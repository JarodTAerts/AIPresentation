Underneath, most companies are software companies.

What these businesses actually are:

  • Banks — a database with a UI. The vault is a row in a table.
  • Insurance — actuarial models + claims-workflow software.
  • Airlines, logistics, retail — pricing, routing, inventory engines.
  • HR, legal, accounting, marketing — workflows on SaaS that is software.
  • Hospitals, schools, governments — the EMR, the LMS, the case-management system.
